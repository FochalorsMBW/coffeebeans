Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. International shipping times vary by location.

What growing conditions do coffee seeds need?

Coffee plants generally thrive in warm, humid environments with temperatures between 60-70°F (15-21°C). They prefer rich, well-draining soil and partial shade. Each variety may have specific requirements which we detail on their product pages.

Do you offer growing guides with your seeds?

Yes! Each order comes with a detailed growing guide specific to the coffee variety you purchase. We also offer comprehensive growing resources on our blog.

Can I return seeds if they don't germinate?

We stand behind the quality of our seeds. If less than 80% of your seeds germinate when following our growing instructions, we offer a replacement or refund within 90 days of purchase.
